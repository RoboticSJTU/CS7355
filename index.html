CS7355: Frontier of Autonomous Driving Technology

Shanghai Jiao Tong University, Spring 2024

Introduction

Autonomous driving, a frontier field combining artificial intelligence and robotics, is rapidly progressing towards commercialization, poised to revolutionize transportation systems. This course at Shanghai Jiao Tong University provides a comprehensive analysis of autonomous driving system architecture and principles, covering AI, machine learning, and robotics fundamentals. Students learn to build autonomous driving modules, apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, and explore cutting-edge research. Prerequisite knowledge in linear algebra, probability theory, statistics, and Python programming is recommended, along with familiarity in robotics, control theory, computer vision, or machine learning.
